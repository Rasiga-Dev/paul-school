Extra-Curricular Activities

Our extra-curricular activities are designed to nurture creativity, build confidence, and develop essential life skills beyond academics. From arts and sports to technology and wellness, students are encouraged to explore their interests, discover hidden talents, and grow into well-rounded individuals.

01

Chess

Chess improves memory, strategic thinking, and logical reasoning. It helps students develop concentration, patience, and decision-making skills through structured gameplay and problem-solving challenges.

02

Yoga

Yoga enhances flexibility, reduces stress, and improves concentration. It supports both physical fitness and mental well-being, helping students maintain balance and focus in their daily activities.

03

Drawing & Creative Arts

Drawing and creative arts encourage imagination and artistic expression. It improves observation skills, creativity, and allows students to visually express their thoughts and ideas.

04

Dance

Dance improves physical fitness, flexibility, and coordination. It also boosts confidence and helps students appreciate cultural traditions and artistic performance.

05

Computer Education

Computer education builds digital literacy and technical knowledge. Students learn computer applications, internet usage, and basic programming concepts essential for the modern world.

06

Communication Skills

Communication skills training develops public speaking, confidence, and leadership qualities. It helps students express their ideas clearly and work effectively in teams.

07

Keyboard Music

Keyboard music training enhances memory, rhythm, and creativity. It improves concentration and develops an interest in music and artistic learning.

08

Martial Arts

Martial arts such as Silambam and Karate build discipline, physical strength, and self-defense skills. It also improves confidence, focus, and overall fitness.
