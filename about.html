About Paul Matriculation School

Paul Matriculation School, located in the heart of Polepettai, Thoothukudi, has been providing quality education since 1983. Our school is committed to nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline, and strong moral values.

We focus on the overall development of students by combining academic excellence with extracurricular activities, life skills, and character building. With experienced teachers and a supportive learning environment, we ensure every student grows with confidence and achieves their full potential.

Montessori Program – Building Strong Foundations

Our Montessori program focuses on early childhood development through hands-on and activity-based learning. Children are encouraged to explore, learn at their own pace, and develop independence in a nurturing environment.

This approach helps build confidence, creativity, and curiosity, giving every child a strong foundation for future learning.
